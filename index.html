Capicúa Studio is more than clothing. It's a love letter to the Dominican street — the colmado where neighbors congregate, the domino table where battles are fought with tiles and wit, the motoconcho winding through Santiago's calles, the reggaeton echoing off concrete walls.

We are the intersection of barrio culture and fashion-forward vision. Every piece carries the weight of Dominican identity — the resilience, warmth, and creative genius of a people who turned nothing into something extraordinary.
